What AIM Does

KEEPING IT SIMPLE.
DOESN'T NEED TO BE COMPLICATED.

AIM runs locally on your desktop — no subscriptions, no internet required, no data sent anywhere unless you choose Xero sync.

📄
Invoices & Quotes
Create professional invoices and quotes with line items, GST, payment terms, and your business branding. Convert quotes to invoices in one click.
🚗
Vehicle Records
Track every vehicle by rego, make, model, VIN, and WOF/service due dates. Full job history per vehicle, linked to the customer record.
👤
Customer Management
Maintain a full customer database with contact details, payment terms, and complete invoice and vehicle history at a glance.
🧾
Bills & Suppliers
Record supplier bills with per-line account coding for COGS or overheads. Track what you owe and when it's due.
📊
Reports
Profit & loss, GST return, receivables, and payables — all anchored to the NZ financial year. Export or print at any time.
📧
Email & PDF
Send invoices and quotes directly from the app via your own email account. Professional PDF generation built in, no printer required.
📸
Job Photos
Attach photos directly to invoices and bills. Document vehicle condition, completed work, and parts for your records.
💾
Local & Backed Up
All data stays on your machine. Auto-backup to a folder of your choice — shared drive, USB, or cloud-synced folder.
👥
Multi-User
Staff and admin roles with separate logins. Admins control approvals, deletions, and financial visibility. Staff see what they need to.

Xero Integration

Your books.
Always in sync.

Connect AIM to your Xero account and your invoices, contacts, and payment status stay in sync — automatically. No double entry, no exports, no hassle.

✓
Push invoices to Xero Approved invoices sync to Xero as authorised — line items, GST, and account codes included.
✓
Pull payment status When Xero marks an invoice as paid, AIM updates automatically. No manual reconciliation.
✓
Sync contacts both ways Customers created in AIM push to Xero. Xero contacts import back into AIM.
✓
Import your chart of accounts Pull your exact Xero account codes so every line item maps correctly — no guessing.
✓
Push supplier bills Bills with per-line COGS or expense coding push directly to Xero as accounts payable.
01
Connect your Xero account OAuth 2.0 login — AIM never sees your Xero password. Secure token stored locally on your machine.
02
Import your chart of accounts One click pulls your Xero account codes into AIM so line items map to the right accounts.
03
Work as normal Invoice your customers in AIM. When you approve an invoice, push it to Xero instantly.
04
Sync payment status Hit Sync from Xero and any invoices paid in Xero are automatically marked paid in AIM.
